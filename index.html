About Us

OpenSio offers innovative software solutions built on stable and pioneering technologies. The company specializes in supporting library software like Koha ILS, Dspace IR and Moodle LMS and custom application development. With a vision to provide quality software solutions to libraries around the world and a strong team of library professionals, we have been successful in bringing in the latest technology to ensure that every project that leaves our pipeline is efficacious.

Why OPENSIO?

We are one of the top leading library automation companies in India and from our inception onwards we have been focusing to devote all our time and resources on providing open-source solutions for libraries where we have experienced, dedicated expertise professionals. And in the meantime, we have gained a deep understanding of how the library or information centers work and what their specific needs are.
